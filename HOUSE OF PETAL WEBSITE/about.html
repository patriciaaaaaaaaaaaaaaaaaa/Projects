About Us

House of Petals

House of Petals Flower Shop accepts any type of flower arrangement for any occasions, including bouquet flowers for a surprise package for your beloved, peace offering flowers, wedding flowers, funeral and sympathy flowers arrangement or stand, and inaugural flowers arrangement with vase and basket arrangement. In the metro Manila area, we offer free delivery and shipping.

The goal of our shop is to provide clients with the best value and experience possible by providing the best variety of plants and floral goods at better cost. Our business also has knowledgeable, customer-focused personnel who deliver prompt, effective services.

We are dedicated to provide only the best and most exquisite floral arrangements, backed by prompt, courteous, and friendly service. All of our customers are important to us, thus our qualified staff is committed to make your experience enjoyable.

Our Florists

For any gift occasions, our florists offer unique, imaginative, and enjoyable suggestions on how to make it special for both of you and the recipient, whoever they may be and for whatever purpose your gift is for. We also offer a wedding bouquet and wedding function flowers services guaranteed to please.
